About Dhoni University

Dhoni University (DU) is one of the leading universities in Bihar, India, providing world-class education and research opportunities across various disciplines. The university is known for its excellent faculty, diverse student community, and state-of-the-art facilities. Located in the heart of Bangalore, it offers a vibrant learning environment and focuses on holistic development through an industry-oriented curriculum.

  • Established: 1992
  • Campus: 25 Acres
  • Courses Offered: B.Tech, MBA, BBA, BCA, M.Tech, and many more
  • Accreditation: UGC, NAAC A+
